Welcome to VNIT Nagpur College Trip program

Introduction

Welcome to the "Home" page! Here, you'll find all the information you need to plan and participate in our exciting college trips. Follow the steps below to get started.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1 click on payment link

Click on the payment link, fill in all the required details, and click the "Submit & Pay" button. You will be redirected to the payment gateway. Please pay only two thousand rupees. Hurry up, as seats are limited!

Step 2 Register for a Trip

Once you done the payment , click on the registration form. Fill out the registration form with your personal information and submit it.

Step 3 Enjoy the Trip

Follow the itinerary and participate in all the planned activities. Stay connected with the trip coordinator for any updates or changes. Most importantly, have fun and create unforgettable memories!
